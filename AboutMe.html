How this idea came to life

This system was designed to eliminate traditional logbooks and optimize studio management. Previously, staff manually tracked duties in paper books, which were frequently misplaced or lost. By replacing physical books with BEATS, offices save valuable workspace and can seamlessly track tasks by date.


Additionally, there was no centralized booking or reporting mechanism for the Conti studio. Staff entered and exited without coordination. With BEATS, staff can check studio availability and secure bookings anytime, anywhere even outside of standard office hours. The system also introduces a digital portal to file broken or missing equipment reports instantly.
